What does NFT stand for?

What is a crypto token?

+ -

Non-fungible means that it is unique and can't be replaced with another thing.


What is a crypto token?

+ -

Crypto tokens are a type of cryptocurrency that represents an asset or specific use and belong on their own blockchain.


What is the blockchain?

+ -

Blockchain is a system of recording information in a way that makes it difficult or impossible to change, hack, or cheat the system. A blockchain is essentially a digital ledger of transactions that is duplicated and distributed across the entire network of computer systems on the blockchain.


What is an nft?

+ -

A NFT is a non-interchangeable unit of data stored on a blockchain that can be sold and traded. NFTs are tokens that we can use to represent ownership of unique items. They let us tokenise things like art, collectibles, even real estate. They can only have one official owner at a time and no one can modify the record of ownership or copy/paste a new NFT into existence.


What can NFTs represent?

+ -

NFTs can represent different things such as art, collectibles, music, domain names, photography, sports, trading cards, virtual worlds or provide utility. MightyMind NFT project is focused on providing utility.
